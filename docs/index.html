Dog Tracker

Track your off-leash dogs in the backcountry using Meshtastic LoRa radios. No cell service needed.

Topo Maps

USGS topographic maps with offline support. Download regions before your trip.

Compass

Big arrow pointing at your dog with distance and fix age. Works without looking at the map.

One-Tap Ping

Request an immediate position update from any tracker — don't wait for the next broadcast.

Fully Offline

No internet required in the field. BLE to your Meshtastic radio, LoRa to your dogs.

How It Works

Attach a small Meshtastic GPS tracker to your dog's collar. Carry a companion Meshtastic node (like a Heltec V3) paired to your iPhone via Bluetooth. The trackers broadcast GPS positions over LoRa mesh every 2 minutes, and Dog Tracker shows them on a topographic map.

Requirements

  • iPhone running iOS 17 or later
  • A Meshtastic radio node to pair with your phone (e.g., Heltec V3)
  • Meshtastic GPS tracker(s) on your dogs running stock firmware with position module enabled
